TOPIC
    PSMilkyWay 0.1.0

SHORT DESCRIPTION
    Module for making it easy to look up different facts about our solar system such as Planets and Moons.


LONG DESCRIPTION
    This module is a collection of PowerShell cmdlets that make it easy to look up different facts about
    our solar system such as Planets and Moons.

    See the help for individual Functions for more details about syntax.
